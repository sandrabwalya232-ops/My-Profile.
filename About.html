About Me

I am a passionate 3rd-year Cybersecurity student at Kapasa Makasa University.

I am a passionate and driven third-year Cyber Security student at Kapasa Makasa University, pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Cyber Security.

With a deep fascination for digital defense and technology, I am committed to protecting the digital world from evolving cyber threats. My passion for cybersecurity stems from the realization that in today's interconnected world, data is the new oil — and protecting it is one of the most critical responsibilities of our generation.

I thrive on understanding how hackers think, discovering vulnerabilities, and building robust security solutions that keep systems and people safe. Whether it's learning about ethical hacking, network security, cryptography, penetration testing, or digital forensics, I am constantly hungry to expand my knowledge and practical skills.

Currently in my third year, I am focused on developing both technical expertise and a strong ethical foundation in cybersecurity. I believe that true security professionals don't just defend systems — they think like attackers, innovate like developers, and act with integrity.

My ultimate goal is to become a skilled Cybersecurity Specialist who can contribute to making the internet a safer place while helping organizations and individuals protect what matters most.
